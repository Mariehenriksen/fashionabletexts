I am a second-year student at Oxford Brookes Univeristy doing a BA in Media, Journalism and Publishing. I find both publishing and journalism very interesting, but I have a slightly bigger interest for Journalism because I have a passion for writing and would love to tell peoples' stories.

About Me

I was born and raised in Norway, more specifically, the north of Norway on the Lofoten islands. I absolutely love going for hikes, especially in the mountains we have right outside our door at home. I have three older brothers who all are married and have children. I am adventurous and I enjoy travelling and exploring the world. Because I have travelled a lot, I have friends spread across the globe. I live in Oxford right now, and I really like it here. I am however missing the nature at home. So, if Oxford was by the coast and had more mountains, it would be the perfect city for me.

My portfolio and website
